Welcome to the wonderful world of Lines 98, it is a simple interesting classical game. However it has a brilliant & cool user interface, we sure you will love it at the first time!

GAME PLAY:

- For LINE mode: make score by arranging 5 or more balls of the same color consecutively in straight lines (vertically, horizontally or diagonally)
- For SQUARE mode: make score by arranging at least 4 balls of the same color to form a rectangle or square
- For BLOCK mode: make score by arranging at least 7 balls of the same color close together.

Imagine you play a famous game in fantastic style!
