Siyakwamukela nezwe elimangalisayo Lines 98, it is a simple ezithakazelisayo game classical. Nokho it has a brilliant & cool interface yomsebenzisi, thina isiqiniseko uzongithanda it ngesikhathi kuqala!

GAME PLAY:
- For LINE mode: ukwenza score ngokuhlela 5 noma ngaphezulu amabhola of the umbala ofanayo ngokulandelana in imigqa iqonde (zibheka, ukuyaphezulu noma ngokuphambeneyo)
- For SQUARE mode: ukwenza score ngokuhlela okungenani 4 amabhola of the umbala ofanayo ukwakha isikwele noma isikwele
- Block mode: ukwenza score ngokuhlela okungenani 7 amabhola of nangebala eduze kwenye.

Cabanga nje udlala umdlalo odumile ngesitayela kumnandi!
Ake wenze izikolo abaningi ngangokunokwenzeka futhi inselele abangane bakho manje !!!
