Siyakwamukela nezwe elimangalisayo Lines 98, kuba elula ezithakazelisayo umdlalo we-classic. Nokho-ke has a brilliant & cool interface yomsebenzisi, thina isiqiniseko uzongithanda it ngesikhathi kuqala!

GAME PLAY:
- Ukuze LINE Imodi: ukwenza isikolo ngokuhlela 5 noma ngaphezulu amabhola of nangebala ngokulandelana e imigqa eqondile (thwi, nendawo noma ngokuphambeneyo)
- Ukuze SQUARE Imodi: ukwenza isikolo ngokuhlela okungenani 4 amabhola of nangebala ukwakha isikwele noma isikwele
- Ukuze VIMBELA Imodi: ukwenza isikolo ngokuhlela okungenani 7 amabhola of nangebala eduze kwenye.

Cabanga udlala umdlalo odumile ngesitayela kumnandi!
Masenze izikolo eziningi ngangokunokwenzeka futhi inselele abangane bakho manje !!!
